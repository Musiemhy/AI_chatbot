In the grand halls of Eldoria, King Alden ruled with wisdom, though his heart carried the weight of past battles. His most trusted advisor, Seraphina, was sharp-witted and always two steps ahead in political schemes. Meanwhile, young Prince Edwin, eager to prove himself, often found himself at odds with the brooding knight, Sir Garrick, whose loyalty to the crown was unshakable but whose temper was feared.

Beyond the castle, in the bustling markets, lived Mira, a clever street thief who survived by her quick hands and quicker wit. She had an uneasy alliance with Rowan, a mysterious scholar who seemed to know more than he let on. In the shadows, the exiled sorcerer Malakar plotted his return, seeking vengeance against those who had wronged him
